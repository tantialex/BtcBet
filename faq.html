  1. How do I place a bet?
    1. Click explore at the top to go to the explore page.
    2. Search for a category to bet on that interests you.
    3. Select a specific bet that you would like to take part in.
    4. Enter your bitcoin address to receive the winnings, the captcha, and decide whether you're going to bet "yes" or "no" on the given bet.     5. Deposit your bitcoins, from python_minimumDeposit BTC to 100 BTC, to the address provided.
    6. Wait and see what the result of the bet is! You will automatically receive a payout if you win the bet.

  2. How do payouts work? Is there a fee?
    • Right when the deposit is made, a python_feePercent percent fee is taken off the deposit.
    • When the bet has finished and a decision has been reached, the administrators will automatically trigger a payout. All the deposits, minus the fees, will be split among the winning betters based on how much each person deposited.

  3. How fast are payments processed?
    • Payments are recorded as soon as one confirmation in the blockchain occurs.

  4. What if I make a bet after betting has closed? What if my transaction was sent before betting closes, but confirms after?
    • BTCBet must receive the funds prior to betting closing. If funds are not received with one confirmation before betting closes, they will not be counted toward the bet, and will be kept as a donation to BTCBet.
    • This is a standard policy among similar betting websites, as it ensures that no fraudulent bets are placed after a bet has been resolved and everyone knows the outcome. It will not be refunded, so ensure that all bets are sent with an adequate transaction fee, and refrain from betting in the hour before the bet closes unless you are certain it will be confirmed with the transaction fee supplied.

  5. What do the statuses of "closed" and "resolved" mean?
    • All bets have an ending date and time. After this date and time, no more bets will be accepted.
    • Sometimes there is a period between when betting closes, and when the resolution of the bet happens. An example is that betting may close before the olympics begin, but a resolution about medalists isn't reached until after the olympics is finished.
    • Following the resolution (when the status has been turned to "resolved" and a decision is displayed on the bet page), there is a 24 to 72 hour discussion period. During this period, people may contact us if they disagree with our posted outcome. Afer the 24-72 hour waiting period, payouts will be made and no further complaints will be accepted.
    • Not all bets close before they are resolved. If a bet is labelled resolved, but it has not yet reached its end date/time, this means that betting has closed early due to the result of the bet being determined early.

  6. What if I'm extremely unlucky and place a bet right as the bet is satisfied, but not as it comes to a close?
    • Your bet will be automatically returned when payouts are processed (minus our fee).
    • If you are trying to cheat us and place a bet after a resolution has already been reached, you may receive a refund. This detail is provided on a per-bet basis, so check the bet's description. In certain cases, we may keep your funds, depending on when exactly the payment was received. Contact us for more information regarding this policy.
